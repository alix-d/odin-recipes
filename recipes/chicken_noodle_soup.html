Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is my grandma's chicken noodle soup recipe. It's very savory and tasty, and I think everyone will like it. Use smoked chicken for even more flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups wide egg noodles
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 12 cups chicken broth
  • 1.5 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp poultry seasoning
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 0.5 cup cornstarch
  • 0.25 cup water
  • 3 cups diced, cooked chicken meat

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and oil, and boil until noodles are tender, about 8 minutes. Drain, rinse under cool running water, and drain again.
